A Peaceful Day at the Beach

The beach is one of the most relaxing places I know. Last Sunday, my family and I spent the whole day at the beach to enjoy the warm weather and fresh air. The sun was shining brightly, and the waves were calm, making it a perfect day for outdoor fun.

We arrived in the morning and quickly set up our spot under a big umbrella. My younger brother and I went swimming, while my parents relaxed on the sand and read books. After swimming, I built a tall sandcastle with a moat around it. It wasn't easy, but it was fun and satisfying.
