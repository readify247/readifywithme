Little Red Riding Hood

One day there was a little girl named Little Red Riding Hood. She was asked by her mother to deliver cake to her grandmother. When she was heading for her grandmother's house, Little Red Riding Hood met a wolf. "Where are you going, little girl?" the wolf asked. "I'm bringing this cake to my grandmother," replied Little Red Riding Hood.

The wolf had an evil plan because he wanted to eat them both. Then he ran through a shortcut to her grandmother's house. After arriving there, he knocked on the door. When Grandma opened it, the wolf caught her and locked her in a closet. The bad wolf then wore Grandma's clothes and laid in bed, waiting for Little Red Riding Hood.

When Little Red Riding Hood arrived, the wolf in disguise told her to come in. She did not realize that it was the wolf. Then the wolf ate Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother. After eating them, the wolf fell asleep beside the river.

Suddenly, a woodcutter came. He saw the wolf's large belly and knew what had happened. He saved them by releasing them from the wolf's belly and filled the wolf's stomach with a big stone. Then he threw the wolf into the river.

Source: Adapted children's tale.
